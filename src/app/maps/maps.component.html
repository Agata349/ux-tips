Empathy Map

It helps team members understand the user’s mindset. We use it to:

  • Build empathy for your users
  • Force alignment and understanding about a user type

When to use it:

  • Beginning of any design process
  • When categorizing research notes from a user interview

Customer Journey Map

It focuses on a specific customer’s interaction with a product or service. We use it to:

  • Highlight specific customer journey touchpoints that cause pain or user's happiness
  • Create one shared, organization-wide understanding of the customer journey

When to use it:

  • At any point in the design process, as a reference point amongst a team throughout a product design cycle

Customer Journey Map example

Customer Experience Map

It generalizes the concept of customer-journey maps across user types and products. It is not tied to a specific product or service. We use it to understand a general human behavior (before a customer journey map in order to gain understanding for a general human behavior).
