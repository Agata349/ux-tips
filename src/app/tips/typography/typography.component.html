Typography

It is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable and appealing when displayed.

We can distinguish four main categories of fonts

  • Serif - classic & traditional - great for printed materials and headings
  • Sans Serif - modern & clean - perfect for websites, widely like by all ages
  • Script - fancy & handwritten - should be use in very limited amounts
  • Decorative - fun & unique - also should be use in small doses, not recommended for serious websites.

Web-safe fonts: Arial, Courier New, Georgia, Tahoma, Times New Roman, Verdana

Tips

  • Keep contrast high
  • Use size 10 or larger
  • Rarely use all upper cases
  • Limit the number of fonts
  • On the web, use web-safe fonts
  • Design for copy and paste
  • When combining, be intentional
