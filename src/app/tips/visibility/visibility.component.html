Visibility

The usability of a system is greatly enhanced if elements are clearly visible to the user. That's why we need to remember about things like: the "fold", scrolling, banner blindness.

Elements which should be placed above the fold

  • Branding
  • Main navigation
  • Contact information, including Help
  • Search box
  • Shopping carts
  • Sign up/login

Users look at: plain text, faces & body parts and unusual things.
